Today's Healthcare Data is Broken.

Your most sensitive information is fragmented, vulnerable, and out of your control, leading to errors, delays, and privacy risks.

Fragmented Records

Patient data is scattered across different hospitals, labs, and clinics, creating an incomplete and unreliable picture of your health.

No Patient Control

Serious Privacy Risks

OUR SOLUTION

MediChain: Your Health, On Your Terms.

We provide a secure, decentralized healthcare record system built on blockchain technology. MediChain gives patients full control over their medical data with a unique mix of world-class security, patient-controlled sharing, and a universal health ID.

  • Patient Ownership: Truly own your health records and choose who can see them.
  • Tamper-Proof Security: Built on blockchain, your data stays secure and private at every step.
  • One Health ID: A single digital identity that works across hospitals, labs, and even countries.

A Simple Process for Total Control

Our platform simplifies health data management into a few easy steps, putting you firmly in the driver's seat.

1

Onboard & Secure ID

Patients register on MediChain to receive a unique and secure digital health identity.

2

Upload & Encrypt

Hospitals and labs upload encrypted medical reports, prescriptions, and scans to a decentralized network.

3

Grant Access

Patients use their private key to grant or revoke access. Doctors only view data with explicit consent.
